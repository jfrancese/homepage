James Anthony Francese

Mathematician & Philosopher



My name is James Francese, and I'm currently a senior undergraduate studying mathematics and philosophy at Chapman University.

In mathematics, my research moves between geometry and algebra, with recent work in the deformation theory of finite-dimensional algebras and problems concerning topological groups. I also recurringly work on areas within mathematical physics.

My philosophical work is mostly in metaphysics and epistemology, with a special focus on causality. I also maintain strong interests in logic and the foundations of mathematics.
